About
A little bit about me
    Born in Rio de Janeiro in 1984 to Brazilian parents, I was raised with a lot of love and affection by my parents. My mother has a degree in Social Services and my father has a degree in Electrical Engineering with an emphasis on Systems Analysis. I have always loved the technology field, I believe it was driven by the relationship I had with my father, so pursuing a career in IT was something I wanted to do from an early age. But when I was young, I really liked the hardware side, assembling and maintaining customized computers with casemods, LEDs, water coolers, and I even participated in casemod competitions. It was when I entered college that I discovered the world of algorithms and programming and I really enjoyed this new journey and at that moment, I was sure of what I wanted for my professional life. Subjects such as data structures, algorithms and relational databases were my favorites and my academic performance made clear my passion for the world of technology and application development. I have experience with several programming languages, such as ASP, Java, and Javascript, but it was with Microsoft .NET, since version 1.0, that I built my career and am here today. Today, .NET is my main language and it is with it that I have the most seniority. Because I have a solid foundation in object orientation, SOLID principles, and because I know several design patterns, I believe that new challenges with other languages ​​can be achieved with a shorter learning curve. In 2024, I was named Microsoft MVP in the .NET category.
